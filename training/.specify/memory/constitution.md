## üé® Visual Design Standards

### Presentation Structure (Based on World-Class Standards)

#### **Typography System**
- **Title Font**: Sans-serif (Helvetica, Inter, Roboto, Space Grotesk)
  - Slide Titles: 36-48px, bold (700)
  - Section Titles: 28-36px, semibold (600)
- **Body Font**: Same family, lighter weights
  - Primary Body: 18-24px, regular (400)
  - Supporting Text: 16-20px, regular (400)
- **Maximum 2 Font Families** per presentation
- **Line Height**: 1.2 for headings, 1.5 for body

#### **Color System**
- **Primary Color**: Brand color for key highlights, CTAs
- **Secondary Color**: Supporting elements, charts
- **Neutral Colors**: 
  - Background: White, off-white (#f5f5f5)
  - Text: Dark gray (#1a1a1a, #333333)
  - Borders: Light gray (#e0e0e0)
- **Semantic Colors**:
  - Success/Growth: Green (#22c55e)
  - Warning/Caution: Orange (#f59e0b)
  - Risk/Negative: Red (#ef4444)
  - Insight/Info: Blue (#3b82f6)

#### **Spacing & Layout**
- **Slide Margins**: 40-60px from all edges
- **Content Spacing**: 24-48px between sections
- **Grid System**: 12-column for complex layouts
- **White Space**: Minimum 20% of slide should be empty

#### **Data Visualization**
- **Charts**: Clean, professional style
  - Remove unnecessary gridlines
  - Clear labels and legends
  - Color-coded for meaning
  - Source citation required
- **Chart Types**:
  - Bar Charts: Comparisons, rankings
  - Line Charts: Trends over time
  - Pie Charts: Market share (max 5 segments)
  - Scatter Plots: Correlations
- **Maximum 1 Chart Per Slide** for clarity

#### **Slide Templates**

**Title Slide**
- Project title (48-60px, bold)
- Subtitle/tagline
- Company logo
- Date and version
- Presenter names

**Section Divider**
- Section number and title (40-48px)
- Brief description
- Full-screen impact design

**Content Slide**
- Slide title (36-48px)
- 3-5 bullet points maximum
- Supporting visual (image, chart, icon)
- Slide number

**Data Slide**
- Clear headline stating insight
- Single chart or graph
- 2-3 key takeaways
- Data source citation

**Comparison Slide**
- Two-column layout
- Clear labels (Us vs. Competitor)
- Visual indicators (‚úì, ‚úó, icons)
- Balanced information

## üìê Slide Composition Rules

### Content Density
- **6x6 Rule**: Maximum 6 words per line, 6 lines per slide (guideline)
- **One Main Idea**: Each slide communicates one key message
- **Supporting Points**: 3-5 maximum
- **White Space**: 30-40% of slide empty

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Primary**: Slide title (largest, boldest)
2. **Secondary**: Key statistics, main chart
3. **Tertiary**: Supporting text, captions
4. **Quaternary**: Source citations, footnotes

### Reading Flow
- **Top to Bottom**: Natural reading pattern
- **Left to Right**: For Western audiences
- **Z-Pattern**: For complex layouts
- **Focal Point**: Use size, color, position to guide eye

## üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes
- ‚ùå Too much text on slides (violates 6x6 rule)
- ‚ùå Unsourced statistics or claims
- ‚ùå Outdated market data
- ‚ùå Overcomplicated language or jargon
- ‚ùå No clear narrative or story arc
- ‚ùå Weak or missing call to action

### Design Mistakes
- ‚ùå Inconsistent fonts and colors
- ‚ùå Low-quality or pixelated images
- ‚ùå Cluttered slides with too many elements
- ‚ùå Poor contrast (unreadable text)
- ‚ùå Excessive animations or transitions
- ‚ùå Misaligned elements

### Market Research Mistakes
- ‚ùå Biased or cherry-picked data
- ‚ùå Ignoring major competitors
- ‚ùå Unrealistic market size claims
- ‚ùå Missing customer validation
- ‚ùå No addressable market segmentation
- ‚ùå Overly optimistic projections without basis
